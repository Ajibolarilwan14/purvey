Purvey

We are a technology-backed social enterprise helping the needy and vulnerable get easy access to affordable and quality food and social support. Purvey is connecting you to passionate individuals and groups that are ready to support you and help you thrive.

Tech Meets Impact

Our goal is to provide and connect 5 million undernourished Africans to a consistent supply of good quality and affordable food by 2025. We do this by connecting food benefactors and those with surplus food to our beneficiaries.

Purvey is built on a blockchain platform to deliver value on a platform of trust.

Why Purvey?

Secure

Purvey is built on top-grade blockchain platform that allows transparency and traceability from benefactor to beneficiary.

Simple

Receive or Give food easily – create a free mypurvey account, select what you want to do and start connecting!


Safe

Purvey is Quality! Our food scientists and quality assurance team ensures only the best is delivered!
